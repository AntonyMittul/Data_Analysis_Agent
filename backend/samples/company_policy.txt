ACME CORP — EMPLOYEE POLICY HANDBOOK (2024)

1. WORKING HOURS
Standard working hours are 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday to Friday. Employees may opt into a hybrid schedule with up to 3 remote days per week with manager approval.

2. LEAVE POLICY
All full-time employees are entitled to 24 days of paid annual leave per year, plus 12 public holidays. Unused leave of up to 5 days may be carried over to the next year. Sick leave is capped at 10 days per year and requires a medical certificate beyond 2 consecutive days.

3. EXPENSE REIMBURSEMENT
Business expenses must be submitted within 30 days with valid receipts. Travel is reimbursed at the economy rate. Client meals are reimbursed up to $75 per person. Expenses above $500 require prior written approval from a department head.

4. REMOTE WORK EQUIPMENT
The company provides a laptop and a one-time $300 home-office stipend. Equipment must be returned upon exit.

5. CODE OF CONDUCT
Employees must maintain confidentiality of client data, avoid conflicts of interest, and complete annual security training. Violations may result in disciplinary action up to termination.

6. PERFORMANCE REVIEWS
Reviews are conducted twice a year (June and December). Promotions and salary revisions are decided during the December cycle.
