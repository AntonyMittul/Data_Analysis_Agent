QUARTERLY BUSINESS REVIEW — Q3 2024

EXECUTIVE OVERVIEW
Q3 revenue reached $4.2M, up 18% quarter-over-quarter and 31% year-over-year. Net profit margin improved to 22% from 19% in Q2, driven by lower customer acquisition cost and higher retention.

REVENUE BREAKDOWN
- Subscription revenue: $3.1M (74% of total), growing 21% QoQ.
- Professional services: $0.8M, flat versus Q2.
- One-time licenses: $0.3M, down 9%.

CUSTOMER METRICS
Net revenue retention rose to 112%. Monthly churn fell to 1.8% from 2.4%. The enterprise segment now contributes 56% of revenue, up from 48%.

REGIONAL PERFORMANCE
North America led with $2.5M (60%), followed by Europe at $1.2M (28%) and APAC at $0.5M (12%). APAC grew fastest at 40% QoQ off a small base.

RISKS & CONCERNS
- Revenue concentration: the top 10 accounts represent 38% of revenue.
- Rising cloud infrastructure costs reduced gross margin by 2 points.
- Two enterprise renewals worth $0.6M are at risk in Q4.

RECOMMENDATIONS
1. Diversify the customer base to reduce concentration risk.
2. Negotiate committed-use cloud discounts to protect margins.
3. Launch a dedicated renewal task force for at-risk enterprise accounts.
4. Increase APAC investment given strong growth momentum.
